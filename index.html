Banana Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • 250 mL Milk
  • 3 ice cubes

Method:

  1. Peel the banana and add it to a blender.
  2. Add the milk and ice cubes to the blender.
  3. Turn on the blender for 30 seconds.
  4. Drink!

Reviews:

  1. Rima: Tasty and simple (I drank it even though I am not fond of bananas)- 9/10
  2. Soumi: Kinda bleh: tasted a little bland (maybe some sugar syrup might do the trick)?- 6/10
